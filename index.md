# Research data management

{% include figure.html img="university-drive.jpg" alt="Students on University Drive, Gold Coast Campus" caption="University Drive" width="75%" %}

Learn steps to: 

- create a functional data management plan
- classify: set up file and folder systems to better store and organise your research data  
- responsible research:  explore useful processes and tools to ensure reproducible ethical research
- store and retain:  find out best practice to store, retain, archive or destroy your data

Learning Outcomes: 

- Creation of a usable and practicle data management plan
- Understand your responsibilities as a data researcher
- knowledge of data management and planning 
- knowledge of how best to store and retrive your data safely
- know who to contact if issues arise

Download Data management planning templates, design naming convetions and set up safe storage accounts
